Cookie Policy – Signyalé Application

1. No advertising, only useful cookies

Signyalé does not use any advertising cookies or trackers for commercial or profiling purposes. The only cookies used serve to:

• Geolocate a product or store at your request, at the time of reporting,

• Ensure the security of the application (e.g.: technical logging),

• Improve navigation (language, session, visual preferences).

2. Geolocation: with your consent only

Location is only enabled when you choose to report a price. No background geolocation is performed.

You are always informed and you can:

• Accept or refuse geolocation at the time of reporting,

• Disable location access in your phone settings.

3. Strictly necessary cookies

Some cookies are exempt from consent because they are:

• necessary for the proper functioning of the application,

• or linked to a service expressly requested (e.g.: displaying a point on the map).

They are kept for a maximum of 13 months, without automatic renewal on each visit.

The cookie policy of the Signyalé application is fully compliant with French and European legal obligations, as defined by:

• GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679),

• Article 82 of the French Data Protection Act,

• CNIL guidelines No. 2020-091 and recommendation No. 2020-092.
